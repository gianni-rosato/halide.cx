Iris

Iris is a fast & efficient image encoder built to help provide a more beautiful Web. Designed around the intricacies of the human visual system and thoroughly evaluated under industry-leading psychovisual metrics, Iris takes Web-first image compression technology to a new level.

Iris for WebP

In its speed class, Iris-WebP is the most efficient image encoder available. Images encoded with Iris-WebP look significantly better than those encoded with the reference WebP encoder, and Iris-WebP's performance outclasses encoders for slower, newer Web-first formats like AVIF. WebP support is also currently ubiquitous, and large websites serve billions of WebP images every single day.

Fast

Iris-WebP's slowest encodes are close to AVIF's fastest, making it faster than competing modern Web codecs while still delivering exceptional quality.

Efficient

Achieve up to 45% better compression than the best JPEG encoders, leading to faster page load times and greatly reduced bandwidth.

High Quality

Iris-WebP is designed with the human visual system in mind, allowing up to 26% better compression than the reference WebP encoder.
